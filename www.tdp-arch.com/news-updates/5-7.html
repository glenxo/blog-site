Friday Five: 417's Best Events 5/7-5/13

Share

For the Mothers

It's time to pull out all the stops and show our moms just how much they mean to us! Shower them with love, food, live music, quality time, and flowers! We have some fun things listed in the Friday Five to make your life easier when trying to decide what to do this weekend! Check it out!

What's Happening This Week?

Ozark Springfest Expo

Where: Ozark Community Center

When: Saturday, May 8th from 9 AM - 3 PM

"**FREE TO THE PUBLIC**Ozark Springfest, presented by Excel Dental, is an annual community event, featuring a business expo, family activities, live music, food trucks, and more!Featuring:

-Bounce houses
-Live music
-Food trucks (food for purchase)
-Win great prizes and giveaways
-Find out about local volunteer opportunities by visiting our nonprofit group booths"

Mothers Day at Little Blue Bird

Where: The Clever Barn

When: Saturday, May 9th @10am

"This event is not just for mother's but it would be a fun place to spend with your momma!!!
*We will have a beautiful fresh FLOWER bar for you to put your own MOTHER'S day ARRANGEMENT together.
*No Baked Cookie Dough food truck will be here with yummy treats!
*We will have live demos from your favorite craft vendors
*Many sales throughout the store
*Samples of our yummiest foodie lines we carry!
*Yummy Macaron Bar
and more.... plan to attend!"

10th EVER Mother's Day Fest

Where: Mothers Brewing Company

When: Saturday, May 9th @ 2-10 PM

Dear lovers, far and near, we are so excited to announce that it is finally THAT TIME! We missed you so much last year that we've decided to have THREE Mother's Day Fests this year. This will help us keep distance between folks while we get back to having fun. We'll cap the guest count at 500, if we're full then you can catch us the next two Saturdays!

Food, Beer, & Live Music!

Horse Back Riding + Winery

Where: Pick up is at Farmers Park - Springfield, MO

When: Sunday, May 9th @10:45 AM - 4 PM

"Join us for a day of horseback riding and wine tasting with a great view of the Ozarks. We will saddle up after a short safety briefing that will let you get acquainted with your horse. We will go on an hour long ride through the fields, woods, and creeks with an experienced guide. This experience is perfect for new horseback riders and experienced riders looking for a relaxing day. The views along the ride will not disappoint you. After, we will drive to Bear Creek Winery, a great family friendly winery, surrounded by fantastic views of the Ozarks. We will enjoy a complimentary tasting of amazing red and white wines. After the tasting, they also have rustic pizza, smoked meats, cheese, and local chocolate for you to choose whatever your heart desires while we relax and socialize at their amazing space. If wine is not your drink of choice, they brew their own beer and it is fantastic!*All riders should be under 250 lbs"

Brew League at Great Escape

Where: Great Escape Beer Works

When: Thursday, May 13th

"For our first time ever, we are hosting Brew League!!! It's a round robin tournament each Thursday in May. From Corn Hole, Ladder Golf, Shuffle Board & Ring toss. There will be prizes for the team that wins the tourney!!Kicks off May 6th at 6:30 till ya win (or lose).Grab a few of your friends, and let's hang out!!Sign up in the brewery and the cost is $25/person and includes a t-shirt!!"
