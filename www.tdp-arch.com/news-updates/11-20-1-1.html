Friday Five: 417's Best Events 1/22-1/28

Share

Spread the Love

January is already on its way out and February is ready to share the love of the holidays with you all. Now is the perfect time to spread love to those around you. Check out this week's blog post to see what you can do to start celebrating now!

What's Happening This Week?

Valentines Mug with Free Hot Cocoa Bomb

Where: Fired Up Pottery and Art

When: Friday, Jan 22nd from 1-3 PM

"$10 plus tax to create a fun Valentines mug, you'll also be gifted a hot chocolate bomb to enjoy in your new mug after it's fired!!."


Grand Opening of Rise Coffee Truck

Where: 900 W Washington St, Marshfield, MO

When: Saturday, Jan 23 at 12 PM

"We are excited to announce our GRAND OPENING! Come expecting quality coffee & great service! We would love if you stopped by on January 23rd starting at 12:00 to support us on this new journey! Lots of love. - Rise Coffee"

Date Night Pottery Wheel Mug Throwing

Where: Art Zone

When: Saturday, Jan 23 from 6-9:30 PM

"Join Art Zone for Pottery Wheel Date Night, Mug Throwing! This is an adults only event with BYOB!
Plan to be with us for about two hours. You'll throw the mug on the wheel then switch to trimming and adding handles before we glaze them. Each participant will create their own mug.
$45 registration is for two people.
6pm has 1 Couples Spots Available
7pm has Sold Out
8pm has 4 Couples Spots Available
9pm has 2 Couples Spots Available
Register online www.artzonenixa.com or over the phone 417-224-9816"

Whetstone Band at Cedar Chandeliers Cafe

Where: Cedar Chandeliers Cafe

When: Friday, Jan 22nd at 6 PM

"Come enjoy awesome food and music!"

Couples Line Dancing - Learn to Two Step!

Where: Marshfield Community Center

When: Friday, Jan 22nd from 8:15-9:15

"Beginners two step class - for anyone looking to add more movement to their week in the new year, for couples looking for a fun evening out, or for anyone looking to learn something new!When: Jan 22, Jan 29th, Feb 5th, and Feb 12th

Where: Marshfield Community Center
Cost: $10 per coupleGrab your dancing partner and your dancing shoes! We'll meet you on the dance floor"
