Friday Five: 417's Best Events 3/6-3/12

All The Freshness

We are in the midst of a month full of sunshine and rain, which means, lots of fresh fruits and vegetables.

*New food truck alert!* You'll have to keep reading to find out when and where! Check out this weeks top events!

What's Happening This Week?

Succulent Class

Where: Schofield + Gray, 358 Hill St, Fordland, MO

When: Saturday, March 7th from 11 AM - 12:30 PM

"We had such a huge response to our succulent class we have added an additional class! Green thumb or not, this class is for you! Please join us on March 7th at 12:30 as Jessi and Micca Looney from Paradise Gardens at Crazy L Ranch teach us different tips and tricks for planting succulents. All you need to bring is yourself! Everything else will be provided, including, several succulents and pots to choose from. The cost is $35 per person, which includes a glass of wine and snacks. These classes will go quickly! To sign up, please direct message us your email address and we will send you a PayPal invoice. Your payment will hold your spot."

We look forward to seeing you!

Grand Opening

Where: Earth's Harvest, 1530E St. Louis St., Springfield, MO

When: Sunday, March 8th from 11 AM - 8 PM

GRAND OPENING ANNOUNCEMENT:

"We are pleased to announce our grand opening will be Sunday, March 8th! Stop by during our opening day between 11-9 for taste-testing, exclusive coupons, and to be entered to win a prize.
100% Plant Based Always 🌱"

2nd Annual Birthday Bash

Where: NEST, 101 E. Church St., Ozark, MO

When: Saturday, March 7th from 10 AM - 5 PM

"🎉COME CELEBRATE OUR 2nd BIRTHDAY!🎉 1st 25 customers :: FREE GIFT! Purchases over $50 :: FREE gift (While supplies last) Buy 2, get 2 on select merchandise 🛑Check this out!!🛑 🙌🏼10-3pm we will offer a stackable discount: -5% off if you join VIP group on Facebook -5% check in on Facebook"

All You Can Eat Pancakes Benefit

Where: Walkabout Coffee Shop, 1860 N Commerce Plaza, Nixa, MO

When: Sunday, March 8th @ 10 AM - 2 PM

Cost: $5 per person - free for children under 3

"We are so excited about our “All You Can Eat” Pancake and Biscuits & Gravy morning. 100% of the proceeds from this offer will be donated to the Least of These, Inc. food pantry! So stop in on and be a positive force for your community!"



Beer & Yoga at 4 By 4 Brewing

Where: 4 By 4 Brewing Company

When: Sunday, March 8th from 11-12 PM

Cost: $15

"Do your friends make you feel guilty for drinking beer before noon?
Now you don't have to carry that guilt! Join us every other Sunday at 11am for a Gentle Vinyasa Flow while enjoying your favorite pint of beer. What more could you need?"
