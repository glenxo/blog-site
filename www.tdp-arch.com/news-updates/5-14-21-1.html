Friday Five: 417's Best Events 5/28-6/3

Share

A Weekend to Remember

It is going to be a sunny and cool weekend here in the Ozarks! We set you up with food, date nights, live music, and an outdoor activity! Don't miss out on all that the 417 has to offer!

What's Happening This Week?

Food Truck Night!

Where: 1751 James River Rd, Ozark, MO 65721-6724, United States

When: Saturday, May 29th @ 3 PM

"Join us at SnoBallz for Food Truck Night! May 29th Chameleon Cuisine is joining us in our lot from 3-9 to serve up some delicious dinner while we serve you up the best dessert in the 417! Lots of seating, games & music!You won’t want to miss out on this"

Maddi Warren at Route 66 Food Truck Park

Where: Route 66 Food Truck Park

When: Friday, May 28th from 6-8 PM

"Join Maddi and Alli for delicious food and jams!"

Finley River Fishing For A Cause Charity Event

Where: Finley River Park

When: May 28 @ 8 AM - May 31 @ 5PM

"Entry Fee for Tournament: $30
EXPO: Free to attend
Get your ticket here: bit.ly/3dabjB8We are so excited to announce the upcoming 2021 Finley River Fishing For A Cause Charity Event! Make sure to mark 'interested' to keep up with updates.Here's more about what it will entail: The Finley River Fishing For A Cause Charity Event will be a three-day event that will be held on Memorial Day weekend, Saturday through Monday. Anglers who participate are required to fish from kayaks or canoes and will submit fish through the CPR (catch, photo, release) method. There will also be a river cleanup held in conjunction with the fishing derby. Anyone can participate in the cleanup - they do not have to be a competitor. The river cleanup participants will receive raffle tickets for a drawing that will be held on Monday during the awards ceremony and expo. Monday's Expo will have an area for local businesses and sponsors to set up and promote their businesses, as well as local organizations such as CC Links and James River Basin Partnership.This Charity Event will directly benefit the upcoming Inclusive Park Playground."

Herb Infused Cocktails

Where: The Workshop at Finley Farms

When: Sunday, May 30th @ 2 PM

"Freshen up your summer sips by learning all about herb-infused cocktails and syrups. Wine and spirits expert AJ Womack guides you through the crafting of a sage and thyme mojito while providing historic insight on syrups and herbs.During the class, you’ll harvest herbs from the farm, make berry and herb simple syrup and whip up a cocktail of your own—all while sipping mojitos.REGISTER: finleyfarmsmo.com/learn

Cost: $55 per person
Age requirement: Ages 21+"


Greek Date Night

Where: Pickwick & Cherry - Fine Foods & Gourmet Gifts

When: Saturday, May 29th & Sunday, May 30 from 6:30-9 PM

"Take your date on a culinary trip around the Greek Isles! We’ll be making Arni me Aginares (stewed lamb with artichokes in egg-lemon sauce), Spanakopita (spinach and feta in crispy phyllo), Horiatiki Greek village salad, and Galaktoboureko, a delicious semolina-custard pastry. After we prepare our feast, you’ll dine while we do the dishes!

Each ticket admits TWO ADULTS. At this time, due to the size of our venue, the nature of this event and ongoing safety concerns, we are only permitting attendees who are fully vaccinated against COVID-19. Please bring proof of vaccination status with you to the event."
