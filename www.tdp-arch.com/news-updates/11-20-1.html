Friday Five: 417's Best Events 12/17-12/24

Share

Merry & Bright

There's something about the glow of the Christmas lights, the good food, great company, and all the other festivities that come with the Holidays! TDP hopes everyone has a very Merry Christmas! Enjoy this week's events!

What's Happening This Week?

Gingerbread House Decorating

Where: Finley Farms in Ozark, MO

When: Saturday, December 19th starting at 2pm

"Join us at The Workshop on December 19th for a fun-filled day of Gingerbread house decorating! With each house purchased, you will receive a variety of toppings and icing to turn your gingerbread house into a candy-covered cottage. Plus, enjoy one complimentary hot cocoa and house coffee to sip while you decorate! Be sure to bring your finished house to one of the crew for a photo!
All houses will be entered for a chance to win a $10 Finley Farms gift card and a bag of our house coffee beans!*Limited gingerbread houses available. Advanced registration is encouraged."

Click here to register!

Holiday Family Swim with Scuba Santa!

Where: Diventures Springfield

When: Sunday, December 20th from 3-5 PM

Make the Holidays a splash this year with Diventures. Bring the whole family for an afternoon of photos with Scuba Santa, family swim, and a crafting event for the kids. Bring a friend, and if they sign up for swim lessons within a week, you’ll get a referral credit of $20!

Call 417.883.5151 to reserve your spot!

Holiday Pop-Up Market

Where: Farmers Market of the Ozarks

When: Wednesday, December 23rd from 4-8 PM

"It’s the event that is sure to boost your holiday spirits! Join us for our Holiday Market on Wednesday, December 23rd from 4pm - 8pm. Complete your last-minute holiday shopping with our artisan vendors and grab the ingredients to make all your favorite holiday dishes.Get ready for a night of safe, local, and festive fun!"

3 Cheers for 3 Years!

Where: 4 x 4 Brewing Company

When: Saturday, December 19th Ave, 12 - 10 PM

"Our Three year anniversary is December 22nd but we decided to party the day & night away with you guys Saturday, December 19th! (12-10pm)The last 3 years have been a blast for all of us involved and we have loved every second, but sometimes it's nice to step back and enjoy what you have done!
If you don't know the story behind 4 By 4 Brewing, here it is:To many people's surprise 4 By 4 is not about 4x4 trucks/jeeps or back roading. 4 By 4 is about family, friends, and community and is owned by 4 Guys and their 4 Wives along with many (9) children in between! 4 By 4 started out as a (very serious) joke in a garage while we watched home brewing at it's finest! The dream became more and more realistic the more we all hung out together and finally on a birthday trip to Nashville (for the brewer) we decided (after many craft beers) to take the jump! We came back and started researching and while it took us about 13 months to get it up and running it has grown far past our expectations thanks to all of you!This will be an all-day event and owners will be present throughout! Check out our events and schedule below:
- All Day Event!
- 1 Big Bundle Giveaway to enter!
- Live Music - Justin Larkin: 6-9pm "

*50% Capacity

Rustic Snowman Paint Party

Where: Main Mercantile - Nixa

When: Friday, December 18th from 6:30-8:30 PM

"Join in the fun at our Canvas Paint Party at Main Mercantile!!! We provide all the paint, brushes, and supplies to complete a 16x20 painting. We will go through step by step instructions to make your own Rustic Snowman painting! Only $35!Do you want to paint your own thing...go ahead! We are not going to limit your creativity. This is a great event for a girl's night out or for a unique date night! Be sure to watch for registration, as with social distancing we can only have 6 participants! Due to Nixa's masking ordinance, you will need to have a mask for this class"

Click here to register!
