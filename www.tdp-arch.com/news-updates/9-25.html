Friday Five: 417's Best Events 9/25-10-1

Share

Fall Has Arrived

Time for all the fun fall festivals, haunted corn mazes, and the adorable decor shops! If you are as excited for fall as we are you won't want to miss out on the activities happening this weekend! Happy first week of Fall!

What's Happening This Week?

Field of Screams Haunted Trail & Forest

Where: Summers at the River, 2142 N Sports Complex Ln, Nixa, MO 65714

When: September 25th & 26th at 7 PM - 11:59 PM

"Southwest Missouri's Totally Redesigned Haunted Trail & Forest. We've acquired another haunted attraction and incorporated it into our over 1/2 mile trail which bends through the forest and along the river. Nothing will be the same as last year. In addition to the redesign of our attraction, we'll also be taking many safety precautions. The safety of our staff and customers is our number 1 priority."

60th Annual Black Walnut Festival

Where: Stockton, MO

When: Friday, September 25th from 11 AM - Saturday, September 6th to 11 PM

"60th Annual Walnut Festival, come enjoy crafts, food, queen contest, entertainment, and parade."

The Maze at Stone Valley

Where: The Barn at Stone Valley Plantation

When: Saturday, September 26th from 10 AM - 8 PM

"Do you love plants as much as we do?? Join us for another plant sale outside of Lucos Pizza!
We will also be offering vegan pastries, cold brew coffee, and matcha lattes. Can’t wait to see you then!"

Fall Pop Up Market at Willow Green Acres

Where: Willow Green Acres, 8169 US Highway 60, Rogersville, MO 65742

When: Friday, September 26th from 12 PM - Saturday, September 26th to 4 PM

"Join us Friday, September 25 and Saturday, September 26 for our Fall POP Up Market at Willow Green Acres in Rogersville Mo.
Be sure to check out all of our outdoor Vintage, Antique, Uniques and Food Vendors Friday from 12-4:30 and Saturday 8-4. Our Antique Mall will be full of Tiques and Fall Fun and is open Friday 9-5 and Saturday 8-5.
Our 10 Acres and Greenhouses host hundreds of Beautiful Trees, Shrubs and Perennials. Stroll through our plant display gardens and find the perfect Mums and Pumpkins on our Pumpkin Path!! The Nursery hours are 8-5 both days. "

Field of Screams Opening Night

Where: The Aurora Maze at Adventure Farm and The Zombie Harvest

When: Friday, September 25th from 5 PM - 10 PM

"Come get lost in our 5 acre maze! At dark we kick off our Field of Screams! So much fun to be had!"
