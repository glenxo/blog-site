Friday Five: 417's Best Events 6/4-6/10

Share

Sunny & 75 (82?)

It is that time of the week again! Time to see what fun things the 417 is bringing to you! You don't want to miss a weekend in the sun!

What's Happening This Week?

Spring Farm To Table Dinner

Where: Urban Roots Farm

When: June 4 @ 7 PM - June 5 @ 6:30 or 8 PM

"Reserve your table for our annual farm-to-table dining experience at Urban Roots Farm on June 4th and 5th - tickets are limited! Our Farm-to-Table Dinner is a socially distanced yet intimate event, designed for groups of 4, 6, or 8. Each party will be treated to a family-style dinner, served directly to their table. Each group will be spaced around the farm and seated among the rows of crops, while local musicians play in the open evening air. Dinner will be cooked by Katie Baker of The Gracious Plate, and will feature Roasted Pork, Grilled Chicken, and Loaded Veggie Kebabs, with a variety of gluten-free and vegan-friendly sides, and an Ice Cream Sundae Bar (vegan options available)!Friday, June 4th - 7:00pm and Saturday, June 5th - 6:30pm (first seating); 8pm (second seating). You can reserve tables for groups of 4 ($200, $50/ea), 6 ($270, $45/ea), or 8 ($320, $40/ea), ticket pricing."

When: Saturday, June 5th @ 2 PM

Location: Roller Park
Showtimes: 2:00 & 4:30 (90 minute run time each show)
Sponsor: Sparta Area Chamber of Commerce
Tickets: Tickets can be purchased at a discounted price ($12 for adults and $7 for children and seniors) before circus day from...
**Hodges Insurance**
**Town & Country Grocery Store**
**Sparta City Hall**
**Mountain Country Propane**
** https://cm-circus.square.site **
SPECIAL ANNOUNCEMENT: Your tickets from the rescheduled 2020 date ARE STILL VALID for entry!
You can also call 1-866-BIGTOP-6 during regular business hours for discounted will call tickets. On circus day, tickets can be purchased at the box office and will be $15 for adults and $8 for children 2-12 and seniors 65 & up. No charge for children under the age of 2. Buy your tickets early and save! A portion of the proceeds will go to support our sponsor --the Sparta Area Chamber of Commerce. Our 90 minute family-friendly show features Big Cats, Unicyclists, Birds, Aerialists, Horses, Daredevils and so much more! Visit cmcircus.com to see photos and find out more information on this year's show"

Cruella Brunch

Where: Alamo Draft House Springfield

When: Saturday, June 5th @ 11 AM

"Order from our tasty new brunch menu – Egg BLT, French Toast, or Breakfast Tacos – and pair with one of our new brunch cocktail classics like Desert Spring Water or the Italian 75. Each are available to order along with our full menu at this special brunch screening .Academy Award® winner Emma Stone stars in Disney’s CRUELLA, an all-new live-action feature film about the rebellious early days of one of cinemas most notorious – and notoriously fashionable – villains, the legendary Cruella de Vil. Cruella, which is set in 1970s London amidst the punk rock revolution, follows a young grifter named Estella, a clever and creative girl determined to make a name for herself with her designs.She befriends a pair of young thieves who appreciate her appetite for mischief, and together they are able to build a life for themselves on the London streets. One day, Estella’s flair for fashion catches the eye of the Baroness von Hellman, a fashion legend who is devastatingly chic and terrifyingly haute, played by two-time Oscar® winner Emma Thompson. But their relationship sets in motion a course of events and revelations that will cause Estella to embrace her wicked side and become the raucous, fashionable, and revenge-bent Cruella"
