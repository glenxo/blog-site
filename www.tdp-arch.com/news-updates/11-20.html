Feeling Thankful

Although we may have another wave of canceled events, the Holiday's are not! Enjoy a mix of drive-through light shows, crafts on the go, virtual turkey trot, and more!

What's Happening This Week?

Virtual Turkey Trot 5K

Where: 2141 E Pythian Ave, Springfield, MO

When: Thursday, November 26th 7 AM - November 29 at 11 AM

Nothing can stop Springfield’s Thanksgiving tradition, even if we can’t all be together this year. The 26th Annual Thanksgiving Day Turkey Trot 5K Run/Walk Presented by Brad Bradshaw MD JD LC, takes place Thursday, Nov. 26 through Sunday, Nov. 29, rain or shine, at the location of your choice.

Register to complete a 5K over Thanksgiving weekend, alone or with friends and family. 🥰 Proceeds from the event benefit the Springfield-Greene County Park Board Youth Recreation Scholarship Fund and the Developmental Center of the Ozarks (DCO).

✨ (Level 1) EARLY BIRD REGISTRATION: by Fri., Nov. 20, 5 p.m.
Adults: $20 • Kids 12 & under: $15

Register online at ParkBoard.org/TurkeyTrot ($3.25 online service fee per person) or download the paper form (deliver to Killian Softball Complex by Nov. 20 or postmark by Nov. 17.)

🎽 Registration includes a long-sleeved Turkey Trot t-shirt, race bib, suggested race routes.

✨ (Level 2) DRIVE-THROUGH PACKET PICKUP: Wed., Nov. 25, 8 a.m.-6 p.m. at Killian Softball Complex.

🚘 ON-SITE REGISTRATION during Packet Pickup:
Adults: $30 • Kids 12 & under: $25

🥫 At Packet Pickup, please drop off ﬁve nonperishable food donations for Ozarks Food Harvest.

✨ (Level 3) RUN/WALK YOUR OWN 5K: Thanksgiving Weekend:
Nov. 26-29. Upload your race time and photos to ParkBoard.org/TurkeyTrot

✨ (New Level) HAVE YOUR T-SHIRT MAILED: Register by Nov. 11 and pay an additional $5/t-shirt to have it mailed anywhere in the US.

📸 #TurkeyTrotSGF • Share your photos in your shirts and race bibs to compete in contests, like largest family, best costume, best dressed pet, etc. Upload your race results: include a photo of your Active Endurance app or other distance tracker or personal GPS app. All race times and contest entries must be submitted by midnight on Sunday, Nov. 29.

Christmas Lights of Ozark

Where: Farmers Park, 2144 E Republic Rd, Springfield, MO

When: Now - January 2nd

Our annual "Drive Through" Christmas Lights show here in Ozark kicks off November 12th with the 15th annual Mayor's Tree Lighting in the Finley River Park. After that the park is open every night from November 13th through January 2nd for the drive-through light show. Driving through is free, though donations are accepted at the end to help build our light show every year as well as fund other Parks needs throughout the year.

Lights are open from 5PM to 10PM Sunday - Thursday, and 5PM-11PM Friday and Saturday.

Special thanks to the Ozark High-school National Honor Society for helping to hand out candy canes, as well as National Junior Honor Society.
