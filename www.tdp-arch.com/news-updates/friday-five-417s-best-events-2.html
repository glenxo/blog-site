Friday Five: 417's Best Events 7/29-8/04

Share

Our Top Picks for this weekend in the Ozarks

Tappin' Roots Music Fest

Tie & Timber Beer Co. 1451 E Cherry St, Springfield, MO

The 2nd annual Tappin' Roots Music Festival begins at 12pm and hosts 6 local bands from 4:00 pm until 9:00 pm. General Admission is $10 per person. Click here for tickets.

Moonlight Madness at Silver Dollar City

Don't have time to visit SDC during the day? Moonlight Madness is the perfect opportunity for you! Enjoy 12 hours of non stop fun for 16 straight days (July 23 - Aug 7). The park is open from 9:30 am until 10:00 pm with extended ride time on over 40 different rides and attractions.

Location: Silver Dollar City Attractions 399 Silver Dollar City Pkwy, Branson MO

Image courtesy: Silver Dollar City

Springfield Botanical Gardens

Location: 2400 S Scenic Ave. Springfield MO

Have you visited the Botanical Gardens yet? At 114 acres, its no small park. With everything from a butterfly house and Lake Drummond or kids playground to various themed gardens and even a Japanese Garden ($4 admission), there is a flower to satisfy everyone's taste. The gardens and grounds are open daily from sunrise to sunset.

The 50/50 Community Dinner

July 30th from 6:00pm - 9:00 pm, Finley Farms, Ozark

This special six-course dinner fundraiser at the beautiful Finley Farms benefits the Springfield/Branson Chef's Association And the Springfield Animal Advocacy Foundation. Tickets can be purchased here.

Dirt 66 Grand Opening at Fellows Lake

Celebrate the Grand Opening of the Dirt 66 Trails at Fellows Lake on Saturday, July 30th. Food and beverages will be available along with a host of other sponsors, raffle items, and activities. Visit their Facebook event page here for more information.
