Friday Five: 417's Best Events 7/24- 7/30

Share

Choose Joy

It's FRI-YAY! There are so many fun events happening this weekend to get excited about. Focus on your joy this weekend! Grab your favorite people and head to any of these events for a fun time! See you there!

What's Happening This Week?

65th Annual Ozark Booster Club Pro Rodeo

Where: Ozark Boosters Club Arena, 1200 N Riverside Rd, Ozark, MO

When: Friday, July 24th from 8-11 PM

"Come out to the 65th Annual Ozark Booster Club Pro Rodeo presented by PFI Western Store, Ozark Chevrolet, and Hollie Estes - Missouri Farm Bureau Insurance. The action begins at 8 pm!
This is a full scall IPRA sanctioned rodeo with bareback, saddle broncs, team roping, barrel racing, and bull riding!
Discount tickets are available at PFI Western Store and Apple Market. Advance Adult tickets are $10, Advance Children tickets $5 (6-12), and 5 and under get in free.
Plus, it's FREE kids Friday night at the Rodeo. Get your free kids ticket at the Apple Market nearest you (while supplies last).
See you at the Rodeo!"

Moonlight Madness

Where: Silver Dollar City Attractions, 399 Silver Dollar City Pwky, Branson, MO

When: Saturday, July 25th from 9:30 AM - 10 PM

"Enjoy over 12 hours of nonstop fun for 16 straight days during Moonlight Madness at Silver Dollar City! We're open from 9:30 am to 10pm, so ride all day and into the night with extended ride time on over 40 rides & attractions, food specials, and a nightly fireworks finale presented by Humana®

In addition, enjoy TWO superstar concerts FREE with your Silver Dollar City admission!
- Aug 1 - NEWSBOYS (6pm & 8:30pm)
- Aug 6 - DIAMOND RIO (6pm & 8:30pm)

If that wasn't enough, you can arrive after 5pm and pay on $30(+tax) per person!"

Click here to get your tickets!

Sizzlin' Summer w/ Lecrae

Where: James River Church, 6100 N 19th St, Ozark, MO

When: Sunday, July 26th w Services at 9:00 AM, 10:45 AM & 12:30 CST

"Join us July 26th as we welcome Lecrae to James River Church for our Sizzlin’ Summer series! You won’t want to miss this incredible Sunday as we come together for a time of powerful worship and an exciting message!

Be sure to share this event to invite your friends and family to join us for this incredible service!

Where to Watch:
Facebook LIVE | YouTube LIVE | Jamesriver.tv"

Raptor Run 2020

Where: Missouri Institute of Natural Science, 2327 W Farm Road 190, Springfield, MO

When: Friday, July 24th from 5 PM - 8 PM

"Bring the family for a night of fun to help raise money for the Missouri Institute of Natural Science and Riverbluff Cave! Participants can run or walk the 5K, or 1-mile Fun Run. Funds from this fundraiser will benefit the museum and aid in continued research within Riverbluff Cave. In addition, donations will aid in partnering with area schools to offer educational experiences to local students on Missouri’s natural history and its fragile ecosystem.

Stick around after the run for free tours of the museum and activities for the kids. Enjoy the museum grounds on this sure to be Spectacular Spring Evening! Complete with fossil dig for kids, popcorn, snacks, and other entertainment."

Golfing for Justice Pro-Am AM & PM Rounds

Where: Millwood Golf and Racquet Club, 3700 E Millwood Dr, Ozark, MO

When: Monday, July 27th from 7:30 AM - 7:30 PM

Cost: $2,750 per team

"Join us Monday, July 27 for the Golfing for Justice Pro-Am at Millwood Golf & Racquet Club. This is a tremendous fundraiser for the PGA Korn Ferry Tour's Price Cutter Charity Championship presented by Dr Pepper, which has gifted more than $16.5 million to Ozarks children's charities since 1990.
Morning registration: 6 a.m. with continental breakfast
Morning tee time: 7:30 a.m.
Afternoon registration: Noon, with lunch
Afternoon tee time: 1:30 p.m.
Play with a pro: Your group can play with a pro.
Great golfer gift pack: Each golfer receives logoed apparel
On-course beverages & snacks: We'll have coolers out on tee boxes, and snacks on your courts
Lunch provided: It'll be in the clubhouse!
Trophies: To the winners

Call: 417-887-3400
