Friday Five: 417's Best Events 5/8-5/14

Share

Beep Beep

Hey friends - We have missed you! Good news! Friday Five is back! We are so excited to slowly be getting back into the swings of things...Just in time for some summer fun! Check out this list of things you can safely participate in. Some may only be from your car but trust me it's worth the field trip!

What's Happening This Week?

Ozark Farmers Market Opening -- Drive-Through Market

Where: Ozark Famers Market at Finley Farms

When: Thursdays from 3-7 PM

"OFM will open May 7th, 3:00-7:00 pm as a Drive-Thru and/or Pre-order Pick-Up shopping format. Customers will interact with vendors through their driver's side window as no customers will be permitted to exit their vehicles. OFM/Finley Farms staff will guide customers to vendors' booths, so come at your preferred time and we'll direct you to your favorite local foods. Maps and vendor lists will be provided upon arrival, and many vendors accept direct pre-orders to maximize your shopping efficiency. Tokens for cash purchases and SNAP/ebt will be available at the HUB tent. Come support locally grown and crafted foods at OFM."

Champagne Brunch and Bubbly Bar

Where: Vineyard Market

When: Saturday, May 9th from 10:30-2

They are back open and ready to serve a fabulous brunch!

"Champagne Brunch and Bubbly bar at the Vineyard Saturday mornings 1030-2 PM. Join us for endless Mimosa‘s endless bloody Mary’s and great fresh fair. "

Fair Food Funday

Where: Ozark Empire Fair, 3001 N Grant Ave, Springfield, MO

When: Friday-Saturday May 8-9th from 11 AM - 7 PM

Foot-long Corndogs - $4
Little Brown Jugs of Root Beer - $4
Root Beer Floats - $3
Cheese Curds - $3
Deep Fried Oreos - $3
Sidewinder Fries - $3

Load up the family and come on out to the Ozark Empire Fairgrounds May 8 & 9 from 11AM - 7PM both days!

Not feeling fried food but the rest of the family is? No worries we will also have our Stockyard Smokehouse open serving BBQ sandwiches and ribs! All below combos are $10 and include one side (tax included in price)!
Pulled Pork Sandwich
Smoked Turkey Breast Sandwich
Smoked Bologna Sandwich

Side Options: Coleslaw - Potato Salad - Chips - Sweet Potato Fries
- Beer Battered Sidewinder Fries
The Stockyard Smokehouse will also be offering beer & wine specials, details to come! "

Monroe House Market

Where: Monroe House Market

When: Saturday, May 9th from 10 AM - 2 PM

"Each month we open our Market location to showcase our carefully curated collection of furniture and home decor. We are bringing you the best in quality and beauty. Whether you are furnishing your home or just need to revamp your space we can help you. We also offer design consultations. This market is on a first come first serve basis so be early for the best selection! Monroe House Market is located in the historical district of Fair Grove."
