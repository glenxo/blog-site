Friday Five: 417's Best Events 1/24-1/30

Share

Humanity runs on coffee.

This Friday we have given you many solutions to how to survive this cold front... Savory pies, fresh hot coffee, networking, sisterhood nights, and entertainment! Check out some events that will keep you warm, while helping you not go stir crazy locked in your house!

What's Happening This Week?

Designed Sisterhood Night

Where: James River Church - South Campus

When: Friday, January 24th from 7-10 pm

Cost: FREE

It’s time to #savethedate for the first Designed Sisterhood Night of 2020 - Friday, January 24th at 7 pm!
•
It’s a UNITED NIGHT!
✨Hosted at the James River Church South Campus in Ozark, MO!
✨Boutique Shopping & Cafe open at 5 pm
✨Auditorium doors open at 6:30 pm
✨FREE event for girls of all ages (10-100yrs)
✨Super-Fun production & surprises
✨Lots of laughter & Sisterhood to share
✨A special message from our very own Pastor Debbie Lindell
•
Invite ALLLLLL your friends. It’s going to be a night to remember!"

Biz 417's Think Summit

Where: Springfield Art Museum, 1111 E Brookside Dr, Springfield, MO 65807

When: Friday, January 25th from 8 am - 430 pm

Cost: Prices may vary

"Join the best local visionaries, creators, leaders, and founders for a day of thought-provoking presentations, networking and interactive participation designed to move our region forward. Biz 417's Think Summit presented by People Centric Consulting Group is a dynamic experience that breaks away from your typical business conference: live entertainment, charismatic speakers, food, co-working and a happy hour to end the day. Surprises, goosebumps, and inspiration guaranteed. It’s time to experience Think Summit yourself."

Savory Mini Pies

Where: The Workshop at Finley Farms

When: Sunday, January 26th from 4:30 - 6:30 pm

Cost: $68 per person

Capacity: 10 People

"As we settle deep into the chill of winter, we all want to have a go-to recipe for a warm comfort meal. Chef Eleanor Taylor will teach you a spin on a classic favorite 一pot pie, using her famous butter pie crust recipe. As you cook down curried vegetable stew on the stove, Eleanor will show you the ins and outs of crust making for your own savory mini pie and you will whip up a salad to go with your meal. At the end of the class you will get time to socialize and relish in what you made in the kitchen with a glass of wine.

Space is limited, must register online to reserve your seat. Click the ticket link to sign up."

Click here for your tickets & to reserve your spot!

Coffee Class at Springtown: Intro to Home Brew Methods

Where: Springfield Coffee Co, 3541 S. Lone Pine, Springfield, MO

When: Thursday, January 30th from 6 - 7:30 pm

Cost: $20 per person

"Are you an avid coffee-shop lover, looking to up your at-home coffee brewing game? Maybe a Keurig user in recovery (we joke, but seriously)? Even if you just enjoy a good cup of coffee and want to know more about what all goes into the perfect cup at home, this may be the event for you!
.
Springtown Coffee Co. will be hosting its very first "Coffee Class" as an introduction to home brewing methods. This is the perfect class for beginners who just want to expand their knowledge of coffee and learn how to better utilize some of the most common at-home brew methods. In this class, we'll be covering the Chemex pourover and the traditional French Press. We'll also be talking about the differences in beans, roasts, grind sizes and all kinds of coffee-related tips!
.
There is limited availability for this class, so don't miss your opportunity to sign up! Tickets are $20 per attendee and include 1.5 hour class and multiple coffee beverage samples."
