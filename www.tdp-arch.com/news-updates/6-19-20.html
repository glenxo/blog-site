Friday Five: 417's Best Events 6/19 - 6/25

Share

Happy Father's Day!

This week is all about the Fathers out there! Show your dads or husbands a little extra love this weekend and take them out for a good time. Check out some awesome Father's Day themed activities going on this weekend in the 417!

What's Happening This Week?

Benefit for Officer Mark Priebe

Where: The Vineyard Market, 1759 West St Hwy J, Ozark, MO

When: Saturday, June 20th from 5 - 9 PM

Benefit for Officer Mark Priebe 21 year Veteran with Springfield Police Department
On Saturday, June 20th, The Vineyard Market along with Cafe B-29 will be hosting a benefit for Office Mark Priebe, who was injured in the line of duty. The event will be featuring a classic car & motorcycle show, live music at 6 pm featuring Resurrection, and a silent auction. Donations for Officer Priebe will be accepted! If you cannot join us please donate to.

https://www.gofundme.com/f/help-the-family-of-spd-officer-mark-priebe

Fathers Day Weekend at Little Beau's Backyard

Where: Little Beau's Backyard, 1123 Keithley Road, Walnut Shade, MO 65771

When: Friday, June 19 - Saturday, June 20 from 1 -4 PM

Cost: $10 a person, kids one and under are free

June 19 & 20 Dads come on out and enjoy watching your kiddos play with our furry friends.

Make smores by the campfire
Pet the farm animals
Play horseshoes and corn hole
Rose the mini donkey should be very close to having her baby donkey

Movie in the Park: Frozen 2 (PG)

Where: Nixa Parks and Recreation, 701 N Taylor Way, Nixa, MO

When: Saturday, June 20th from 8 - 11

Cost: FREE

All movies are held at McCauley Park, begin at dusk, and all movies are FREE! Please bring lawn chairs or blankets. Concessions will be available for purchase. All movies are dependent on the weather. No smoking and no alcohol. Please remember to social distance while attending the Movie in the Park.

Father's Day at Dickerson Park Zoo

Where: Dickerson Park Zoo, 1401 West Norton Rd

When: Sunday, June 21st from 9 AM - 5 PM

"Celebrate Father's Day at Dickerson Park Zoo. Free admission for all dads on Sunday, June 21."

Dad's Escape for Free!

Where: Springfield Escape Room, 3554 S Campbell Ave, Springfield, MO

When: Friday, June 12th from 10-11 AM

"This Father's Day, every Dad will get to escape one of our rooms for FREE- so come celebrate your Father this Sunday!"
