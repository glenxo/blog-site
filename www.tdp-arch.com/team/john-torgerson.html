John Torgerson

/ AIA | NCARB

John is our fearless leader and founder and he's someone we all to look up to. His natural ability to connect with everyone around him is something we all strive to duplicate. On a daily basis, we witness his fun, quirky, smiling personality. John is passionate about his high school sweetheart of 31 years, their 3 girls, and his best pal Gunner (a 1-year old spunky bird dog). When he's not at work, you could probably find him down at the cabin hunting, or down at Table Rock Lake enjoying the sun. He loves his 'fur grandbabies' but can't wait for some actual grand kids to spoil.

More About John

John is a long-time resident of Ozark and is a graduate of Hammons School of Architecture at Drury University. He has more than 20 years of architectural experience and specializes in large, complex projects and project management. As the founder of Torgerson Design Partners, John currently serves as President responsible for firm operations—leading projects with the constant idea to evaluate rational solutions, re-think the obvious, re-work the complicated, and practice simplicity.

John has set the bar high for client satisfaction and is growing the firm with a long list of loyal clients. His mission is to educate the staff and to set a powerful example that will move the firm forward and create a legacy.

John is licensed in 12 States, along with the 31 firm State licenses.
