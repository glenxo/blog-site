Cathy King

/ NCIDQ

Some may say Interior Designer, we say Super Woman! From starting out as the girl who took shop instead of home economics, to the woman who earned her degree, to the mom who has homeschooled all 3 of her children, Cathy somehow still found the time to start her own business (then we stole her). Did we mention she has the strength to work with her husband Aaron? You know...the man with the frog. See, Super Woman.

More About Cathy

Cathy's 20+ year experience has earned her bragging rights when it comes to all things interior design.

Originally from Farmington Missouri, Cathy is now one of our amazing interior designers at Torgerson Design Partners. Cathy went straight into the field after graduating from Southwest Missouri State University. She has spent many years developing a diverse portfolio ranging from Healthcare, education, corporate spaces, and hospitality venues. With her unique experience, she is able to provide finish and furnishing selections, as well as, interior space planning, preparation of permit documents, and architectural detailing.
