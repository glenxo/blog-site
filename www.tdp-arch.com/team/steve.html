Steve Brumfield

This Louisiana raised draftsman is our all-American family man. Steve enjoys sitting on the front porch visiting with friends, family, and neighbors, loves quality time with his wife going to flea markets and antique stores; collecting Coca-Cola merchandise! He loves vacationing to Disney World and has gotten his family (2 daughters and 4 grandchildren) hooked on the tradition! You can find steve enjoying dinner at either Tequila's 2 and Big D's BBQ or watching the latest Star Wars movie!

More About Steve

Born and raised in Lousiana, Steve is a man of many trades. He spent his summers growing up helping on his grandfather's farm, worked on offshores drilling rigs for more than 10 years, drove an 18-wheeler for a couple of years, after finding his passion he has spent the last 25 years in drafting and design. Steve is an excellent communicator and strives to always build a good rapport with clients and contractors.

Education:

Drafting and Design Certificate from Delta-Ouachita Regional Technical Institute
