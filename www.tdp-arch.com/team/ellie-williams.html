Ellie Smith

This adventurous woman applied for a job in a new state and once received the offer packed up and moved to a new state in a camper - and are we glad she did! She loves to sing, create new things, help those around her, grow friendships, and spending time with her finance. She has a passion for furniture and exploring antique shops and farmers' markets - I have a feeling we will be seeing lots of cool treasures in the near future.

More About Ellie

Ellie grew up attending a small school near Kansas City, MO where she sang in musicals and played sports. Upon graduating high school, Ellie followed in her Dad’s footsteps when she chose to study architecture. During college, Ellie made close friends, went on mission trips, and discovered a love for all things furniture. After graduating from Kansas State University in May of 2021, Ellie packed up her camper and moved to Ozark. She is looking forward to new friendships, getting in involved with TDP and the community, and exploring local farmers' markets and antique shops. Ellie is actively pursuing her NCIDQ certification!
