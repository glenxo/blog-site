Jeff Masters

Jeff is our bright-eyed, optimistic, people-loving, ever-creative designer. He is an adventure bug and is recharged by the sun and outdoors. (He says he wishes he could live in swim trunks...) He finds happiness in his friends, family and best friend/wife Sheryl. He always strives to make the best of every day. Disclaimer: His favorite artist is Celine Dion (but don't worry, this a judgment-free zone).

More About Jeff

Originally from the Greater Kansas City area, Jeff has been a proud resident of Southwest Missouri since 1988. Over the past 30 years, Jeff has worked in the fields of Architecture, Interior Design, Construction and Project Management. His career has spanned across big box retail, hospitality and resort design, with special emphasis in large aquariums, aquarium life support systems, corporate design, space programming, themed architecture, and master planning.

His design and management beliefs are simple. A great design should render a strong emotion from its end-user (the client), in which this emotion must begin with its author who has to be passionate about their craft and never loses sight of their client’s needs.

In his personal life, he and his wife enjoy spending time with their adult children, he is an avid runner and is involved with his church, life group, and local charities.
